About Connect-R-Us

Connect-R-Us take out the headache of group projects-Meeting up, assigning tasks, and viewing tasks and allows users to enjoy all the features needed for a successful group project in one website!

Features

Here are some of the features Connect-R-Us offers!

Form Teams

New project? Make a team where everyone can have one place to account for each other progress, see which meeting time work best, message each other, and more!

View Task

Check out your tasks assigned for your group project and what other's need to do as well! create new tasks, or assign one to a teammate.

Message

Need to send a quick message or make a note of something? Make use of your message tab to communciate to your team members or make a note to yourself!

Timeline

Visually see what needs to be done before the due date. Take it day by day or week by week!

Fill out Schedule

Fill out your availbility and view other teammates' availability to plan project meetings time.
